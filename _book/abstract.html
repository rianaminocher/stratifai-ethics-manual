Abstract This document describes the plan for ethical and trustworthy design of the STRATIF-AI platform. The plan focuses on translating ethical principles defined by governing bodies into actionable steps tailored to the STRATIF-AI project’s specific needs. In collaboration with diverse stakeholders and in line with current regulations, we have derived a set of comprehensive, feasible and auditable requirements. With the goal of proactively identifying and mitigating ethical risks, our plan is intended to enhance the STRATIF-AI project’s societal impact and trustworthiness.
